## Project Ideas Considered

### 1. Expense Tracker

Target audience:

Students, families, and small business owners who want a simple way to track spending and understand where their money goes.

Main features:

- User registration and login
- Add, edit, view, and delete expenses
- Create and manage expense categories
- Track monthly budgets
- Filter expenses by date, category, or amount
- Dashboard showing spending totals
- Basic spending reports or charts

Status:

Selected as the team's project idea.

### 2. Task Manager App

Target audience:

Students or workers who need to organize personal tasks and deadlines.

Main features:

- Add, edit, view, and delete tasks
- Set due dates
- Track task status
- Organize tasks by priority or category

### 3. Study Planner

Target audience:

Students who need help planning study time, assignments, and academic goals.

Main features:

- Add courses or study subjects
- Create study sessions
- Track assignments and due dates
- View upcoming study plans

### 4. Book Review App

Target audience:

Readers who want to track books and share reviews.

Main features:

- Add, edit, view, and delete books
- Add ratings and reviews
- Organize books by genre or status
- Search/filter by title, author, or rating

### 5. BYUI Group Study App

Target audience:

BYU-Idaho students who want to find or organize group study sessions.

Main features:

- Create study groups
- Add study sessions
- Join available groups
- View group details, meeting times, and subjects
